About Parcel My Box

Parcel My Box is a courier service provider committed to making shipping simple, reliable, and affordable. We specialize in handling packages of all sizes with care and deliver them worldwide.

We bridge the gap between senders and receivers with a seamless and efficient delivery system for all your shipping needs.

How It Works

1

Book Your Order

Enter your shipment details and schedule a pickup or drop off at our center.

2

Packing Recommendations

We provide you with the exact packing supplies needed for your items (box, tape, thermocol, etc.).

3

Home Pickup & Final Courier

Delhivery agents pick up your packed items and ship them to our Hyderabad hub. We check and dispatch via FedEx, DHL or UPS.

Item Restrictions

✅ Permitted Items

  • Clothing and textiles
  • Electronics and appliances
  • Books and printed material
  • Beauty items (non-liquid)
  • Bath items (soap, shampoo bars, etc.)

⚠️ Restricted with Certifications

  • Wooden items (FSC certification)
  • Electronics (CE certification)
  • Food items (FSSAI certification)

❌ Prohibited Items

  • Flammable liquids and gases
  • Weapons and ammunition
  • Illegal substances
  • Perishable items without proper packing

Volumetric Weight Calculation

Formula: (Length × Breadth × Height in inches) ÷ 139 = Volumetric Weight in kg
Example: For a box size 27 x 14 x 13 inches → (27×14×13)/139 = 35.3 kg approx.

Customers can also shop online from Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, Ajio, etc., and provide our Hyderabad delivery address. We receive all items, consolidate and ship them abroad.

Shipping rate: ₹4000 per 500g or ₹4800 per kg (approximate for medicine).

Home-Based Customer Process

If you already have items at home, you can book your order online. Once booked, we provide packing recommendations (box, tape, thermocol, etc.).

After packing, just inform us — our logistics partner Delhivery will pick up your parcels and send them to our Hyderabad office.

After checking, we forward the items internationally using FedEx, DHL, or UPS.
